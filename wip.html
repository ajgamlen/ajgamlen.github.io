Work in Progress

publication
(2024)

Work in Progress is a love letter to the convergence of visual art and music, a carefully curated collection of stories, concepts, and ideas that flow through a diverse range of creative projects.

Each page is a testament to the flashes of creativity, personal reflections, and artistic growth that emerge through collaboration. Together, the writings and artwork trace the intersections between individual experiences and shared expressions, inviting readers to find connection and resonance across creative disciplines.

The result is a raw, experimental snapshot of creativity in motion, celebrating the ways in which different mediums, perspectives, and talents can come together to form something greater than the sum of their parts.

True to its title, the publication itself is a work in progress—an evolving exploration of creative exchange.
