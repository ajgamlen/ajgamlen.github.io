Shooless

web, identity
(2024)


This project was driven by a curiosity to understand the relationship between visual culture and music, two of my main sources of inspiration. Researching this interplay informed the creation of a cohesive and emotionally resonant visual identity for Shooless, a 5-piece genre-fluid band from Aotearoa, NZ. Central to this project was the development of a website and electronic press kit to strengthen Shooless’ brand and market presence.
