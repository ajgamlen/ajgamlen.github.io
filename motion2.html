Intermission

Motion
(2023)


Creative Approach


Conceptual Development: Inspired by liminality and the emotional layers of nostalgia, the project incorporates elements of abandoned architecture and dreamlike environments.

Narrative Design: The story unfolds through a seamless journey, beginning in a tangible, relatable setting (the video store) and transitioning into abstract, surreal spaces (the pool rooms). This progression mirrors the fragmented and fluid nature of memory.

Animation Style: The animation employs a mix of realistic and stylised 3D design, with attention to atmospheric lighting, textural details, and environmental storytelling.

Sound Design: A subtle, ambient soundscape complements the visuals, enhancing the immersive quality and emotional tone of the journey.

Visual Style



Storyboard

From a first-person perspective, viewers are guided through the eerie beauty of an abandoned video store. As the project unfolds, the boundaries between reality and imagination blur, leading the viewer to unexpected spaces tucked away in the store's hidden corners.

Technical Execution











Reflection

My creative journey explored visual metaphors, transformations, and atmospheric elements to capture the essence of liminality. Subtle film grain, vignettes, and nostalgic references added authenticity and emotional depth. Despite challenges in animation and rendering, adapting camera settings and creative post-production solutions enriched the final output. Compromises on quality and dimensions became opportunities to enhance the theme. This project taught me valuable lessons about balancing detail and time, which will guide my future motion design work.
